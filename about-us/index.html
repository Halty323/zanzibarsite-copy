Who We Are - About Us?

Founded in 2014, our company has been at the forefront of construction, furniture manufacturing, and import services, delivering high-quality projects to Zanzibar. As the developer and manufacturer of The Blue Zanzibar, we have successfully contributed to the architectural and infrastructural development of the region. Now, we are Hidden Garden, bringing our third major project to life, further solidifying our commitment to excellence.

We take pride in sourcing premium materials from Turkey, ensuring that our window and door manufacturing meets the highest industry standards. Our dedication to quality and innovation has allowed us to establish three management offices across Zanzibar, providing seamless project execution and customer service.

Looking ahead, our vision extends beyond Zanzibar. We plan to expand our operations into Tanzania and, subsequently, form strategic connections with neighboring countries. By broadening our network and operational scope, we aim to redefine the construction landscape in the region, setting new benchmarks in quality and design.

At the heart of our company lies a passion for building not just structures, but lasting legacies. Join us as we shape the future of Zanzibar and beyond.
